About Semey city

A city of regional significance in Kazakhstan, in the East Kazakhstan region, located on both banks of the Irtysh River. The city of Semey itself, without rural districts subordinate to it, occupies an area of 210 km2. It is the largest city in Kazakhstan, which is not the center of a region or a city of republican significance, although from 1782 to 1997 it was the center of a county (district) and a region (province).
